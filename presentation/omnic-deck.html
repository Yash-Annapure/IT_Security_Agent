03 · How It Works

Six stages. One verdict you can stand behind.

Every uncertain finding gets a reason, not just a score — and a human always gets the last word.

01 PARSE

Read the repo

uv.lock, package-lock.json, requirements.txt — or generate a real SBOM.

02 LOOKUP

Local, offline cache

Matched against a fully-synced NVD + CISA KEV cache. No live calls, no rate limits.

03 IDENTIFY

Resolve the real vendor

Cross-checked against the package's own registry homepage — collisions caught before they're findings.

04 SCORE

Confidence, explained

A trained classifier rates each match; SHAP attaches a reason to every one it isn't sure about.

05 TRIAGE

Nothing dropped silently

Escalated · confirmed · review · rejected.

06 REPORT

Plain English + receipts

What it means, how to fix it, and exactly how much of NVD was actually searched.
